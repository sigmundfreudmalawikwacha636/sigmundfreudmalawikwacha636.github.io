This platform helps store owners understand their sales performance. You see trends, identify top products, and track revenue through clear charts. The tool works on your computer to process sales files and show results in your web browser.

⚙️ System Requirements

Your computer needs the following to run this software:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11.
  • At least 4GB of RAM.
  • An active internet connection for the first-time setup.
  • A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

📥 How to Install and Start

Follow these steps to set up the dashboard on your machine:

  1. Visit the official repository page to download the project files.
  2. Locate the green "Code" button and select "Download ZIP".
  3. Extract the ZIP folder to a convenient place like your Desktop.
  4. Open the extracted folder.
  5. Locate the setup file named "run_app.bat" and double-click it.
  6. Wait for a black window to trigger the loading process.
  7. Once finished, your web browser will open automatically and display the dashboard.

📈 Features

The dashboard provides several tools to help your business decisions:

  • Sales Overview: See total revenue and order counts for specific periods.
  • Data Filtering: Use simple menus to look at specific regions or individual products.
  • Trend Analysis: View line charts that show if your sales move up or down over time.
  • Category Breakdown: See which items bring the most profit to your business.

📁 Preparing Your Data

The software works with CSV files. To see your own data, ensure your file has columns for Date, Product Name, Category, and Total Price. Place your data file inside the folder named "data" located within the main project directory. The dashboard updates automatically when you select your file from the dropdown menu inside the application.

💡 Troubleshooting

If the application does not open, check these common items:

The window closes immediately: Ensure your computer is connected to the internet, as the system downloads necessary components during the first run. Check your antivirus settings to ensure it allows the script to run.

The web page shows an error: Refresh your browser page. If the issue persists, close the black command window and double-click "run_app.bat" again to restart the service.

Data does not appear: Confirm that your file is a CSV format and that it lives in the correct "data" folder. Ensure column names match the expected format described in the data preparation section.

🛡️ Privacy and Safety

Your data stays on your local computer. This software processes information only on your hardware. No sales data leaves your machine or travels to outside servers. You maintain total control over your business records.
